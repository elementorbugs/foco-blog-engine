Privacy Policy

FOCO does not sell your data. The mobile app stores task content locally on your device by default. The website collects standard analytics (pages visited, time on page) to know what's working. We comply with GDPR and CCPA. Email support@tryfoco.com to access or delete your data.

Last updated: 2026-05-21. This policy applies to the FOCO mobile application, the tryfoco.com website, and any free tools hosted there.

1. What data we collect

Website (tryfoco.com)

We collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, referrer URL) and analytics events (pageviews, time on page, scroll depth, click events). This is used to understand which content helps readers and which content fails them.

If you sign up for the newsletter, we collect your email address. If you contact us via email, we receive your email content and email address.

The free tools (focus timers, brain dump, body doubling room) operate entirely in your browser. What you type into the brain dump never reaches our servers. Timer state lives in your browser session only.

Mobile app

The FOCO mobile app stores your task content locally on your device by default. We collect anonymous usage events (which screens you visit, which features you use, how long sessions last) to improve the product. We do not read the content of your tasks.

If you create an account, we store your email address and authentication credentials. If you subscribe, App Store / Google Play handle billing — we receive a subscription status flag, not your payment details.

The app may request optional permissions (microphone for voice capture, notifications). You can deny these at any time without losing core functionality.

2. How we use your data

To deliver the product (let you log in, sync data across devices if applicable, send transactional email like password resets).

To improve the product (understand which features get used, which content gets read, where users get stuck).

To respond to your inquiries (when you email us, we use your email to reply).

To send marketing email — only if you explicitly opt in to the newsletter. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in every email.

To comply with legal obligations (responding to lawful subpoenas, tax records).

3. What we do not do

We do not sell your data to third parties.

We do not run display ads or share data with advertising networks.

We do not read the content of your tasks, your brain dump entries, or your notes.

We do not track you across other websites.

4. Third parties we use

Analytics: we use a privacy-respecting analytics provider for aggregate website traffic patterns. No personally identifiable information is transmitted.

Hosting: the website is hosted on Cloudways. The app uses standard cloud infrastructure (specifics depend on platform).

Email: transactional and newsletter email is sent via a standard email service provider.

Payment processing: subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store and Google Play. We do not handle credit card data.

If you need the exact current list of subprocessors, email support@tryfoco.com and we will send it.

5. Your rights

You have the right to access the data we hold about you, correct it if inaccurate, delete it, restrict our processing of it, and receive a portable copy. These rights apply globally; GDPR and CCPA make them legally enforceable in the EU/UK and California respectively.

To exercise these rights, email support@tryfoco.com. We respond within 30 days, usually faster.

6. Cookies

The website uses functional cookies (to remember session state) and analytics cookies (to count visitors). We do not use advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser; some site features may break if you do.

7. Children

FOCO is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, email support@tryfoco.com and we will delete it.

8. International transfers

FOCO operates from Israel; some service providers operate from the EU and US. Data may be transferred internationally to deliver the product. We rely on standard contractual clauses where required by GDPR.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security practices: encrypted connections (HTTPS), encrypted storage where appropriate, access controls on our infrastructure. No security is perfect; we will notify affected users within 72 hours of any breach involving personal data.

10. Changes to this policy

We update this policy when our practices change. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the current version. Material changes will be announced via the website and, if you have an account, via email.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns, email support@tryfoco.com. For EU/UK GDPR matters specifically, this is also our data protection point of contact.

Note: this policy is a starting template. Before publishing, have a lawyer familiar with your jurisdiction (Israel, EU, US) review it against your actual data practices, your hosting providers, and any specific app permissions you request.
