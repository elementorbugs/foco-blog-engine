Why this price

$19.99/month is in the middle of the ADHD app market. Goblin Tools is free. Inflow runs $15-20/month. Tiimo runs roughly $10/month for the base tier. FOCO is priced where it is because we are building a narrow tool for a specific moment, and the math works for an independent team that does not sell data or run ads.

The annual price ($99) is what we believe is fair for someone committing to twelve months. It is not a loss leader designed to lock you in — you can cancel anytime within the trial and pay zero.

If price is the only blocker and the free tools on this site cover what you need, use those. They are not a strategy to upsell you. They are the genuine free tier.

Try it. If it doesn't help you start — don't pay.

That is the actual promise. The 7-day trial gives you a week to test FOCO on real tasks. If you do not break the freeze in that week, do not pay. We will not chase you, guilt-trip you, or auto-renew you into a year you did not want.

If you pay and then realize it is not for you, email support@tryfoco.com within 30 days with the subject "Support: refund request" — we process refunds without interrogation.

What you get vs. what we don't do

FOCO does not run display ads. We do not sell your task data to third parties. We do not have a "premium tier" that locks core features behind a higher price. The whole app is one tier — Monthly or Annual is just billing cadence.

We also do not gamify shame. No streak punishments. No "you broke your 47-day record" notifications. The app stays present and helpful in the bad days, not punishing.

FAQ

Is there really a free trial?

Yes. 7 days on both Monthly and Yearly tiers. No charge during the trial. Cancel before day 7 and pay nothing.

How do I cancel?

Through the App Store or Google Play (whichever you bought through) — they handle billing, so cancellation goes through them. Open the store's subscription settings, find FOCO, tap cancel. If you get stuck, email support@tryfoco.com.

What if I pay and then it doesn't help?

Email support@tryfoco.com within 30 days with subject "Support: refund request" and we will refund the charge. No interrogation, no upsell attempt.

Can I upgrade from Monthly to Yearly?

Yes. Cancel the Monthly subscription in your App Store / Google Play settings, then resubscribe on the Yearly tier. The store handles the rest. Existing data stays attached to your account.

Can I downgrade from Yearly to Monthly?

You can switch when the annual term ends. Mid-year switches are handled by Apple/Google's standard prorating — usually they let the annual term complete, then renew as monthly. Email support if you need to do this mid-term.

Is there a family plan?

Not yet. If you and a partner both want FOCO, each subscribes separately for now. Family plans are on the roadmap once we hit critical mass on individual subscriptions.

Is there a student discount?

Not yet. Many students find the website tools (free) cover what they need for studying. If you want the full app and the price is genuinely blocking, email support@tryfoco.com and explain — we have discretion.

Does FOCO sell my data?

No. The mobile app stores task content locally on your device by default. Anonymous usage analytics help us improve the product. Full details on our privacy policy.

Why isn't FOCO free like Goblin Tools?

Goblin Tools is a phenomenal free option built by a single developer. Their model works for them. FOCO's model — small team, independent, no ads, no data sale — requires paid subscriptions to fund development, content, and support. We are honest about that math.
